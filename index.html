PROFILE OF GREAT AMBITION SCHOOL COMPLEX

Great Ambition School is a private school that founded by a Great man by name Sir Jamal in the year 1996 with only four children. The school started as a Nursery school and by the grace of God, the school can now boast two thounsand students from the nursery to the Senior High. The school is located in the middle of Tamale Metropotan assembly. Our SHS remains the most competitive in the Northern Ghana.


The school, GAS was formally called Future Leaders before its current name. It was formally located in Sagnarigu sub metro. As the student population kept on increasing, hence the need to relocate its present location.

The school has enough facilities to accomodate a larger number of students. Our Computer laboratory, science laboratory, and Home economics laboratory remain the envy of all the public schools in the region. The school has a bording facilities for both male and female as well as school bus for staff and students. In sum, the school in second to none in the region with the sole aim of producing graduates who the seasoned knowledge and skills for advance studies and employment.


We have good toilet facilities for all students in the schoo. Constant water supply and a good drainage system making the school the most cleanest school in the region. The school cadet and the sport team have trophies constantly for the last fours years. In looking for a quality and affordable school for your ward, then look no further than the Great Ambition School Complex.
